Safety Guidelines

1. Use the Emergency Alert Button

In case of immediate danger, press the Emergency Alert button. This will instantly notify your family and emergency contacts for assistance.

2. Keep Emergency Contacts Updated

Always ensure your emergency contact list is up-to-date in the app. This helps authorities or our team reach your loved ones without delay.

3. Locate Nearest Police Stations

Use the Police Stations feature to identify nearby stations. Head there if you feel unsafe or require immediate official support.

4. Contact NGO Helplines

Reach out to women's rights NGOs in your area through the app. They offer legal, psychological, and emergency support for women in distress.

5. Use Mechanic Locator if Stranded

If your vehicle breaks down, use the Mechanic Locator instead of waiting in unknown or unsafe places. Help will come to you quickly.

6. Plan Fuel Stops in Advance

Avoid getting stranded late at night by using our Fuel Stations feature to find and stop at well-lit, secure stations during your trip.
